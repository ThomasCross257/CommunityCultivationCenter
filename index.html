Welcome.

Welcome to the official website for Community Cultivation Center ("CCC"). CCC Strives to help the local community by bringing together people from all walks of life; be they in need of socialization, a hobby, a boost to their self-esteem, or perhaps faced with circumstances that a purposeful task will improve an outlook. This community garden project will allow our community "cultivators" a chance to connect with others in a common goal. By partnering on a common goal of growing food and plants that nurture the body and spirit, we believe there lies a power in an agrarian experience that can help individuals socialize and builds bridges. Cultivating plants to share for food and for beauty can develop healthy relationships with others and the environment.
